# BRAND.md — Memora Brand Identity

## One-Line Brief
"A photo book brand that feels like your most aesthetic friend's
Instagram — but make it a product."

---

## Audience
- Age: 18–28 (Gen Z core, elder millennial adjacent)
- Identity: Romantics, travelers, memory-keepers, film lovers
- Behavior: Chronically online but craving something tangible
- Mindset: Anti-corporate, values authenticity over polish
- Pain: Their best memories live trapped on a phone screen

---

## Brand Personality
| Trait        | Expression |
|--------------|------------|
| Romantic     | Soft blush, italic serif, tender copy |
| Wanderlust   | Sky blue accent, travel imagery, open layouts |
| Nostalgic    | Film grain, warm tones, candid photography |
| Minimal      | White dominates, never cluttered |
| Slightly raw | Dashed borders, offset shadows, slight rotations |

---

## Voice & Tone

### Copy Rules
- Short sentences. Never more than 12 words per line.
- Instrument Serif italic carries the emotional weight.
- Clash Display carries the confident, bold statements.
- DM Mono carries functional, factual UI text.

### Words TO use
- memories, moments, hold, printed, chapter, film, candid,
  doorstep, together, yours, real, tender, wander, bound

### Words to NEVER use
- revolutionize, seamless, powerful, innovative, leverage,
  platform, solution, streamline, optimize, cutting-edge

### Headline Formula
Clash Display bold statement + Instrument Serif italic softener
Example:
  "Every photo"         ← Clash Display 700
  "deserves a home."   ← Clash Display 700, blush underline

### Subtext Formula
Instrument Serif italic, muted color, 17–20px, max-width 400px
Example:
  "From weekend trips to wedding days — Memora turns your
  camera roll into something you can actually hold."

### Label/Tag Formula
DM Mono 11px, letter-spacing 0.2em, blush pill or dashed border
Example: "[ your memories, printed ]"
Example: "✦ 2,400+ books printed · Ships to Egypt & GCC"

---

## Brand Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Film grain on images | Clean studio photography |
| Candid, warm, real photos | Posed, corporate stock photos |
| Slight card rotation | Perfect grid alignment everywhere |
| One coral CTA | Coral used as accent throughout |
| Whisper of blush or sky | Both blush AND sky in same section |
| Dashed borders for soft elements | Rounded corners above 8px |
| Offset brutalist hover shadow | Drop shadows or glow effects |
| Centered italic serif quote | All-caps shouting |

---

## Emotional Arc of the Page
User lands → feels seen (hero speaks their language) →
gets curious (marquee pulls them in) →
trusts the product (why memora, editor) →
falls for it (themes, testimonials) →
acts (pricing CTA)

Each section should advance this arc. Never break it with
corporate or transactional language.
