## Copy Rules (enforced)
- Never exceed 12 words per headline line
- Subtext max-width: 400px (forces natural line breaks)
- Quotes feel personal — first name + city only, no full names
- ✦ symbol used for all decorative list separators
- Labels always lowercase inside brackets: [ like this ]
- Prices always include currency: EGP 299 (never just "299")
- CTA arrows: → not > or >> or ›
